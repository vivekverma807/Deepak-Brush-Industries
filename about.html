About Deepak Brush Industries

40+ years of precision engineering and manufacturing excellence.

Our Story

From a Small Workshop to India's Trusted Brush Manufacturer

Founded in 1985 in Mangol Puri, Delhi, Deepak Brush Industries began as a small workshop with a big vision — to manufacture world-class industrial brushes that Indian factories could rely on.

What started with a team of 5 dedicated craftsmen has grown into a state-of-the-art manufacturing facility employing over 150 skilled professionals. Today, we serve more than 1,200 clients across 15+ industries.

Our founder, Mr. Deepak Verma, brought decades of engineering expertise and an unwavering commitment to quality that continues to define our company culture.
